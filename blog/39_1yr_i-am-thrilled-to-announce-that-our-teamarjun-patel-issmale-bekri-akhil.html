I am thrilled to announce that our team—Arjun Patel, Issmale Bekri, Akhil Pallem, and I—has been honored with the Best Project and App Award in our COM S 3090 class, the app development class! This achievement reflects months of unwavering dedication, teamwork, and relentless effort from my team and me.

CyDine is an innovative app designed to assist college students in making healthier food choices, addressing the common challenge of the "Freshman 15." By offering personalized meal plans based on Iowa State University’s dining hall menus, CyDine helps users align their nutritional needs with their preferences, fitness goals, and dietary restrictions. The app goes beyond calorie tracking and physical activity logging by fostering a sense of community through a message board where users can share meal ideas and tips. CyDine simplifies healthy eating for students, empowering them to maintain a balanced diet throughout their college journey.
