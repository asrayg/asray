made this really quickly for my family in india last night: aahar.asraygopa.com

one of my family friends pays a nutritionist to tell them how they can improve their meals. it got me thinking about how most nutrition advice doesn't really work for the way indian families actually eat.

nobody is weighing food or counting calories. they're eating rice, roti, dal, sabzi, dosa, and whatever was cooked that day.

so i built a whatsapp agent where you can send a picture of your food and get suggestions on how to make it healthier.

the goal isn't to tell people to stop eating indian food. it's to help them make small improvements to the meals they're already eating.
