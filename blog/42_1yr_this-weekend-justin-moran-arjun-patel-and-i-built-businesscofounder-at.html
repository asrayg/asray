This weekend Justin Moran, Arjun Patel, and I built BusinessCofounder at HackMidwest in Kansas City.

BusinessCofounder is a web platform designed to support new tech founders by addressing two key challenges: acquiring non-technical business skills and finding a non-technical cofounder. The platform uses AI-driven tools for business, legal, and pitch document creation, as well as providing tailored learning experiences. It offers insights into essential business areas such as marketing, finance, operations, legal, and fundraising, helping tech founders navigate the complexities of scaling a business without needing deep expertise in these domains.

I'm also proud to share that we placed 4th in the Machine Learning Connect4 Bot Challenge out of 25 teams, where we built a bot that could strategize and play Connect4.

From optimizing AI models for document creation to crafting a seamless UX, the journey wasn't easy, but the team effort was amazing. Huge thanks to Justin and Arjun for their incredible collaboration throughout the weekend.
