Excited to share that Arjun Patel, Justin Moran, and I won First Place in the technical track at the Ivy Hackathon!

We spent the day developing VerifyAI, an innovative AI plugin designed to protect users from falling victim to deepfake phone scams. Scammers often use social media videos to replicate voices (we were shocked at how easily we replicated Justin’s voice using just the third link we found!) and impersonate loved ones in urgent, high-stress scenarios—like pretending to be in jail and asking for money. VerifyAI offers a simple yet powerful solution to detect and alert users if a call may be a deepfake, helping safeguard families and friends from these increasingly common scams.

I’m so grateful for the opportunity to collaborate with Arjun and Justin—two incredible teammates I’m lucky to have met at ISU. I couldn’t have asked for a better team, and I can’t wait to see what’s next for us!

A huge thank you to everyone at the Iowa State University - Ivy College of Business and the event organizers for creating such a fantastic experience.
