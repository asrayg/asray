with my final first day of school coming up tmrw, I decided to open-source a couple things to help out iowa staters.

https://lnkd.in/gffUnuNJ – accessplus (rip) used to let you download your coursework calendar to import into outlook and google calendar. workday doesn’t do that :( this fixes it

https://lnkd.in/g3qjwWVm – a small python script that checks iowa state’s course search api and sends a discord alert when a seat opens. i get early registration due to a few factors, but most of my friends didn't so this was for them.

https://lnkd.in/gMhmV2BR - scraper to get club officer emails. this was meant to help increase outreach for resource organizations on campus
