Launching The Brik at My First Market

I’m thrilled to share that I officially launched The Brik at the Iowa State University Jingle and Mingle Holiday Market this past Friday. This was my first-ever sales experience, and it couldn’t have been more rewarding.

The Brik is something I’ve been working on since my freshman year—a compact everyday carry tool designed to keep essentials like keys, wallets, and even a phone stand all in one place. It’s built for anyone who values staying organized while on the move. I designed and manufactured everything myself.

Seeing people check out The Brik, ask questions, and make their first purchases was an incredible experience. Selling something you’ve built from the ground up is a feeling I’ll never forget.

Thank you to everyone who stopped by, supported, and shared feedback. This is just the beginning!
