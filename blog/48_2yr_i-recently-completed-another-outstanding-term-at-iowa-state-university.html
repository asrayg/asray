I recently completed another outstanding term at Iowa State University Student Government. I served as one of the three Liberal Arts & Sciences senators, alongside Evan Martinez and Muhammad Azhan. We collaborated with the LAS council and represented LAS students to the fullest extent.

Yesterday, during the inauguration for the 24-25 term, I was honored with the Service Award. This award is bestowed upon individuals who have shown remarkable dedication to Student Government through extensive hours, service across multiple semesters, completion of numerous projects, and consistently being a positive force within the organization and on campus.

Over the past year, I authored significant student legislation that was passed on the Senate floor, served as a legislative ambassador, engaged with state legislators at the Capitol, had a voice in the rules committee, and strived to bring about positive change at Iowa State.

I am eagerly anticipating another fruitful year at Iowa State University Student Government, this time as an Off-Campus Senator. I can't wait to embark on more projects. There's still much to accomplish to truly make an impact on the students' lives.
