It was great time competing at the ISU Innovation Prize and coming in first Place in the AgTech Field. I would like to thank my team for the great pitch and innovations we made.

I worked on making a MVP for the software and hardware for the wildfire detection program. The hardware side included a loRA based post using a hub of environmental sensors and connected in an synchronized array of low frequency radio waves. The software side included a website and an app that gave updates, data, and mapping information.

This issue was something I saw that needed fixing as some of my relatives live in California and I get a bit worried whenever I see an alert for wildfires in their area.

I had a great time pitching and I hope to do more competitions set by the ISU Pappajohn Center for Entrepreneurship.

Watch out for future news for Scalar News. I am making developments on the algorithm and will hopefully get it up and running by the end of the year
