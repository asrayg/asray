I, along with my team of Alex Cecil and Rebecka Larson, won first place at the Ag Innovation and Value Creation Competition hosted by the Iowa State University - College of Agriculture and Life Sciences.

We introduced "Crop Model AI," an open-source AI software application that uses data-based analysis to advise farmers on how, when, and where to best manage their cropland to maximize their ROI.

The competition prompted us to consider the future of agriculture with this question: "How will artificial intelligence be used in agriculture to improve data-based analysis and decision-making?" and we did so.

I learned a lot about agricultural processes and AI/ML models during the competition preparation and can't wait to innovate more in the field of agriculture in the future.
