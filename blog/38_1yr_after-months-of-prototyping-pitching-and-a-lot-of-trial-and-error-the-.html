After months of prototyping, pitching, and a lot of trial and error, The Brik is officially launching today.

After losing my keys and wallet pretty much every day during freshman year, I wanted to create something that would keep everything together — and make sure it never got lost again. It’s an everyday carry tool designed to hold your essentials in one place, and it’s equipped with a tracker.

The Brik started off as a huge cardboard prototype, and now it’s pocket-sized and does everything I imagined when I first came up with the idea.

Appreciate all the support so far. Would love for you to check it out: the-brik.com and follow us on our social media platforms.
