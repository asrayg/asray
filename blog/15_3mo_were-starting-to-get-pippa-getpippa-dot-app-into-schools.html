We’re starting to get Pippa (getpippa [dot] app) into schools

Justin and I built Pippa: an AI powered app where you feed a virtual pet by taking pics of your meals. It helps busy students stay consistent with eating and can also support eating recovery in a low-pressure way.

We’re at ~500 downloads and ~250 daily active users so far.

If you know anyone in a psych department, dietitian network, or school administration (high school or college), I’d really appreciate an intro.

If you had a good relationship with admins at your high school, feel free to connect us too.

Appreciate it 🙏
