Excited to dive deeper into my entrepreneurial journey by working on projects I'm truly passionate about! I’ve applied for buildspace S5 and am revisiting Scalar News, a project close to my heart. Scalar News delivers concise, 60-word news updates to keep you engaged with current affairs.

Originally built in high school, Scalar News grew to millions of yearly readers and impressions with a team of 20 writers. Now, I’m bringing it back with even more passion, leveraging Large Language Models to personalize your news reading experience while keeping you informed.

This has been a long-time dream, and Buildspace is providing the perfect opportunity for me to push myself and create something I’m deeply committed to. Excited for what's ahead!
