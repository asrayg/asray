Along with Arjun Patel and Anoop Boyal, I have helped restart the Algorithmic Trading Club at Iowa State University. We tabled at ClubFest today and received strong interest from students who want to learn more about trading, finance, and programming.

This year, we are planning strategy and coding development sessions, mini and semester-long projects, industry networking and field trips, trading competitions, and more.

If you are a current Iowa State student interested in finance, trading, or programming, send me your email and I will add you to our mailing list. We meet every Tuesday at 6-7PM.

Thank you to the alumni who shared their experiences and supported us in getting this club restarted.

To my network: if you can connect us with professionals in the field for speakers, networking events, or site visits, please reach out. Recruiters, consider Iowa State students in this club for future opportunities.
