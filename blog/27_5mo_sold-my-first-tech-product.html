Original post opener: sold my first tech product.

sold my first tech product.

safebite (safebite.suppersquad.app/) is a website that creates allergen-friendly menus by letting you simply upload photos of your menu. the site quickly generates a clear, allergen-safe menu that’s hosted online. i built it after a friend with allergies spent a lot of time trying to figure out which restaurants were safe to eat at, since many restaurants don’t provide clear allergen menus or information.

just heard the new owner ported it over to their domain. Congrats to SupperSquad on the launch. if you’re a restaurant owner or in the industry, consider joining. it only takes a couple of minutes to be more friendly to the allergen community, which represents about 20% of missed customer opportunity.
