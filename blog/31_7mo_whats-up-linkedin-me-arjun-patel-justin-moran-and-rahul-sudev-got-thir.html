what's up, linkedin! me, Arjun Patel, Justin Moran, and Rahul Sudev got third place at swanhacks 2025.

we built apexnurse; a multimodal ai system integrated into a nurse’s face mask. basically think the newest ray-ban meta glasses, but built for nurses.

it continuously captures audio and video to detect medical emergencies, security threats, and anomalies during patient interactions. alerts are routed to the right specialists with eta estimates. nurses can say “jeff” to activate an ai voice agent powered by recursive rag with hospital-specific context, translation, and task support. after each session, ApexNurse generates a clinical report that is accessible through a mobile app for documentation and review.

this was my first project that was a hardware-based project, and I'm glad I got to try it out at this competition. shoutout father for getting me the raspberry pi for my 13th birthday; finally got to use it. we were able to do hardware, firmware, software, computer vision, 3d printing, appdev, and machine learning at this hackathon. also i was able to make a new library for this hackathon: pip install waveshare-transparent-oled

so MIRA, Brilliant Labs, Mentra, and Meta, maybe it's time to make a product for nurses?
