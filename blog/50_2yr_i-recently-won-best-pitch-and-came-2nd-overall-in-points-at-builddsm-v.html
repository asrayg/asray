I recently won Best Pitch and came 2nd overall in points at BuildDSM v1, a 24 Hour Student Hackathon.

My team and I developed vetU. vetU is an AI/ML powered application for veterinary telehealth, designed to assist pet owners in assessing their pets' health conditions remotely. The application utilizes image recognition technology using Roboflow's API, AI analysis, and access to veterinary resources to provide preliminary diagnoses and recommend appropriate actions. We were able to develop a MVP that could detect sickness in dogs.
