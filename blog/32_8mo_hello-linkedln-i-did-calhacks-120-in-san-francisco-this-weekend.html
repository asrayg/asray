hello linkedln! i did calhacks 12.0 in san francisco this weekend!

got to build profAI, with Rahul Sudev and Arthur Lemus, a real-time accent translation and transcription tool that helps students clearly understand lectures from professors with strong accents by converting their speech into a clear neutral american accent. it also provides instant lecture notes and a smart study assistant that can answer questions using course materials. got to use LiveKit for their cool audio agent tools and Letta for their infinite context windows.

our project: https://lnkd.in/d7z7q3aH

sf highlights: got to meet a lot of cool startups/people, the golden gate bridge☀️, rode in a waymo🚘 for the first time!, silent disco at the rotunda at the palace of fine arts, and checking out the cool public libraries in sf (shoutout the librarians at golden gate valley!!).
