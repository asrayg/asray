I love pitching; it’s probably my favorite part of building startups. I’ve done 30+ competitions, mostly regional, but finding new ones is always a pain. Most lists are outdated or incomplete. So this morning, while procrastinating, I built my own.

PitchPost, pitchpost.vercel.app, is a simple site that tracks pitch competitions, lets you see what’s open, what’s past, and even keeps track of the ones you’ve applied to.

If you’re in the entrepreneurship world or run a competition, there’s a way to submit new ones, too. I’ll keep maintaining it while I’m in college.
