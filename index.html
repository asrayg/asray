About

I'm Asray Gopa, a computer science student at Iowa State University building at the intersection of software, markets, and real-world systems. I'm drawn to problems that involve scale, uncertainty, and decision-making — from prediction market data infrastructure to consumer hardware products.

I've built and shipped projects ranging from The Brik, a physical consumer product sold to hundreds of customers, to trading and data systems that process live orderbook streams. I care about building things that are technically rigorous, economically grounded, and actually used.

Long term, I want to work on systems where engineering meets incentives — financial infrastructure, data platforms, and intelligent decision systems. I'm less interested in building features. I'm more interested in building leverage.

CS Department Independent Study · Spring 2026
